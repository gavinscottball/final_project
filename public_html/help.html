How To Play

Tap the space bar to perform a jump.

Hold the spacebar to perform a long jump.

Get pushed back? Use the right arrow key to restore your position.

Hitting spikes will kill you, ending the game.

Walls cannot directly hurt you, but they can push you out of bounds, ending the game.

The game sepeeds up as you progress, so pay attention and stay sharp!

The longer you stay alive, the higher your score!.

View your high score and others scores on the leaderboard!
